# Roadmap — complete

Every phase below shipped and was verified in the field. This file is
kept as the record of the plan; nothing on it remains open.

* **Phase 1 — inventory & safety spine**: scan/reconcile, duplicates,
  quarantine, journal, backups, profiles, troubleshoot. ✅
* **Phase 2 — package awareness**: DBPF index parsing, conflicts with
  load-order winners, thumbnail extraction (PNG/JPEG/DDS/DST),
  reference-exact CASP subcategories. ✅
* **Phase 3 — provenance & updates**: CurseForge fingerprints, the
  attributed name radar, re-verify, the archive-aware updater with
  closed-door pre-flags and post-update truth. ✅
* **Phase 4 — authorship**: the DBPF writer; manual and creator-first
  automatic merging, journaled and reversible. ✅
* **Phase 5 — the finish**: creators roster, working dashboard,
  history with images, self-healing backups, and the polish pass. ✅

Design boundaries — edges, not promises — are listed in the README.
